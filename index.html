    What makes a strong password?

    • Length ≥ 12-16 characters
    • Mixture of upper/lowercase, digits, and symbols
    • No common words: password, qwerty, admin
    • Avoid personal info: names, birthdays, phone
    • Prefer passphrases: river-cactus-moon!79

    Example Ratings

    • admin123 → Very Weak
    • Qwerty!234 → Weak
    • Orbit!7-sage-harbor → Strong
